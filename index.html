About me

I’m Valentina, a full stack designer with ten years of experience in the field. Right now I work as an independent product designer, building websites and web apps from static mockups to their implementation in the browser. A solid knowledge of basic design principles is the foundation of my design method, together with curiosity and a hefty hunger for knowledge.

My tools

Researching, sketching, Photoshopping, the browser, testing. Iteration is everything. Sublime Text is my best friend, I know my way with GitHub and the terminal; HTML, CSS, and Stylus preprocessing are my everyday tools. I love–and loath!–CSS to the point of writing Waffle, my own framework. When I’m not lost in the editor, I’m playing around with Illustrator or testing new drawing styles.

What is music to me

I am a music nerd. I grew up in a depressed area of Italy, where a free independent radio run by guys and girls from the neighbourhood–on a voluntary basis–was the only way to escape the post-industrial gloom. I identify with the DIY community, I go to gigs on a weekly basis and I write She Does Scream, a blog on female frontend bands, that me and my friend Dana will soon turn into a rad label & booking agency.

Why I love Bandcamp

The moment I signed up for Bandcamp, it completely changed the way I listen, consume, buy and discover music. (I’ve already told elsewhere before, how much I love you) Free streaming, ‘name your price’, redeem codes: I was stoked. I admire how Bandcamp is empowering both fans and musicians, letting the audience directly supports the artists they love. Because, how many times music saved my life?

Why you should hire me

I'm a skilled visual designer

With a MA in Visual Communication, I developed style guides, identities, offline and online layouts, illustrations, lettering and icons for now ten years. I worked three years as a type-designer assistant, and I hold a MA in Typography & Book Design. I'm versed in history of letterforms and I'm an expert in setting type on a page. Any kind of page.

I am a perfectionist. My best work will always be ahead of me.

I design in the browser

Being tired of being stuck in Photoshop, I took over learning HTML, CSS, Stylus, Git. I consider them as any other design tool; designing in the browser had a huge positive impact on my workflow.

I was in charge of the design from mockups to implementation on two web apps; Devcharm, a versioned open library of resources for devs, and Isoverse, a publishing tool for visual artists. Both sites run on Waffle, my own CSS framework.

I have a rich, diverse background

Photography was part of my art education and I've been working with motion graphics. I have extesive experience in printing and special finishings. That’s how I know that printing an artwork with the same colours on different types of paper will result different (even if it’s black and white. Especially if it's black and white.)

I believe that “Cultural awareness is the most important quality of the good designer.”
